Explore Puttalam

Puttalam Beach

A picturesque beach with golden sands and clear waters, ideal for swimming and sunbathing.

Kalpitiya

A popular destination for kite surfing, with strong winds and calm waters, perfect for enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Puttalam Wildlife Reserve

A sanctuary for various species of birds and animals, offering safari tours to explore the diverse wildlife.

Ankurathenna

An ancient Buddhist site with significant historical and religious value, featuring ruins and a monastery.

Puttalam Town

The commercial center of the district, offering a mix of modern amenities and traditional markets.
