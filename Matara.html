Matara Beach

A beautiful beach with calm waves, perfect for relaxation and watersports.

Weherahena Temple

A Buddhist temple known for its large Buddha statue and peaceful surroundings.

Dondra Head Lighthouse

The southernmost point of Sri Lanka with a historic lighthouse and stunning views of the coastline.

Maduwanwela Walawwa

A historic mansion and heritage site showcasing colonial architecture and Sri Lankan history.

Hummanaya Blowhole

The second-largest blowhole in the world, where seawater spurts out dramatically from the rocks.
