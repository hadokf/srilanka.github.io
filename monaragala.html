Explore Monaragala

Yala National Park

Famous for its wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and a variety of birds.

Buduruwagala Temple

A historical Buddhist site with ancient rock carvings and statues dating back to the 10th century.

Kataragama Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site for Buddhists, Hindus, and indigenous people, located in the heart of Monaragala.

Uva Province

Monaragala is part of the Uva province, known for its beautiful mountains and natural beauty.

Ella Rock

Hike up to Ella Rock for panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and tea plantations.
