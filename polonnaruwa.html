Explore Polonnaruwa

Royal Palace of Polonnaruwa

Ruins of the ancient royal palace, showcasing impressive architectural remains from the medieval period.

Gal Vihara

Famous for its beautiful rock-carved Buddha statues dating back to the 12th century.

Parakrama Samudra

An ancient man-made reservoir created by King Parakramabahu I, offering a scenic view and historical significance.

Lanka Thilaka Temple

A beautiful and well-preserved temple from the 12th century, renowned for its artistic design and historical value.

Siva Devalaya

A Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its intricate stone carvings and spiritual importance.
