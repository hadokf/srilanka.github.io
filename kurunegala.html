Explore Kurunegala

Kurunegala Lake

A serene and picturesque lake offering a relaxing escape with scenic views and a peaceful atmosphere for picnics and boating.

Ibba Kanda

Known for its scenic hilltop views, this is a popular spot for hiking and nature walks, offering spectacular views of the surrounding landscape.

Rangiri Dambulla Temple

An ancient Buddhist temple complex with impressive cave temples and a rich history, offering an insight into Sri Lanka's spiritual heritage.

Habarana

A gateway to the cultural triangle, Habarana offers a blend of wildlife and ancient ruins, making it ideal for safari and cultural tours.

Sigiriya Rock

One of Sri Lanka's most iconic landmarks, Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress offering breathtaking views from its summit.

Mihintale

A significant Buddhist site, Mihintale is known for its historical importance and its panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Sapugaskanda

Located in the heart of Kurunegala, this ancient temple is a hidden gem with quiet surroundings and cultural significance.

Dambulla Cave Temple

Located near Kurunegala, this is one of the best-preserved cave temples in Sri Lanka, featuring beautiful murals and Buddha statues.

Mayadunna Falls

A hidden natural treasure, this waterfall is tucked away in the forest and is perfect for hiking and refreshing swims in nature.

Bellanwila Wildlife Sanctuary

A nature reserve known for its variety of birdlife, this sanctuary is ideal for birdwatching and nature walks.

Mullaitivu Beach

Famous for its stunning coastline, this beach is perfect for unwinding and taking in the beauty of Kurunegala's coastal nature.

Kurunegala Pure Mountain

A hidden mountain offering incredible views of the surrounding area, popular with trekkers and nature lovers.

Kandyan Cultural Spring

A cultural landmark where traditional Kandyan dance and rituals are often performed, showcasing the vibrant cultural heritage of the region.
