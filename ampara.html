Explore Ampara

Arugam Bay

Known as one of the best surf spots in Sri Lanka, Arugam Bay offers pristine beaches and a laid-back atmosphere for beach lovers and surfers alike.

Kumana National Park

A nature reserve offering diverse wildlife, including elephants, leopards, and numerous bird species. It's a great spot for nature lovers and wildlife photographers.

Panama Beach

With its crystal-clear waters and tranquil environment, Panama Beach is the perfect escape for those looking to relax in an untouched natural setting.

Surfing in Ampara

Ampara is a hub for surfing enthusiasts, offering a range of beaches with great waves for beginners and experienced surfers alike.

Sithulpawwa Ancient Monastery

A historic Buddhist temple located on a hilltop, offering scenic views and a peaceful atmosphere for meditation and reflection.

Ruwanwella Ancient Site

An ancient site of historical and cultural importance, featuring ruins that date back to the early kingdoms of Sri Lanka.

Pothuvil Temple

A revered temple located in a scenic coastal area, popular for its tranquil surroundings and historical significance.

Black Wild Boar Sanctuary

Home to the rare black wild boar, this sanctuary offers a great opportunity to see these elusive animals in their natural habitat.

Poruvazhy Falls

A stunning waterfall located deep within the forests, perfect for those looking to explore nature and enjoy a refreshing swim.

Lunugamvehera National Park

A lesser-known national park with vast savannahs, home to wildlife including elephants and rare birds. Ideal for safari enthusiasts.

Siyambalanduwa Hot Springs

Relax in the therapeutic natural hot springs of Siyambalanduwa, surrounded by lush greenery and a tranquil atmosphere.

Maha Oya Sand Dunes

Explore the unique sand dunes along the Maha Oya River, offering a fascinating landscape for photography and nature walks.

Nautical Museum

A museum showcasing the maritime heritage of Sri Lanka, with exhibits on shipwrecks, fishing traditions, and sea exploration.

Kallar Bay

A hidden gem of a beach with pristine sands and crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming and picnicking.

Villages of Ampara

Visit the charming villages of Ampara to experience rural life, traditional agriculture, and friendly local communities.
