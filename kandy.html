A vast botanical garden with diverse plant species and stunning landscapes, perfect for nature lovers.

A nature reserve with trails, ideal for bird watching and hiking, located close to the city center.

A giant statue of Buddha on a hill, offering panoramic views of Kandy and the surrounding areas.
